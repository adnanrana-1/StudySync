About StudySync

What is StudySync?

StudySync is an innovative platform designed to transform the way students collaborate and study together. It bridges the gap between individual learners by facilitating connections, organizing study sessions, and building a community of motivated learners.

Key Features

  • Smart Partner Matching: Find study partners based on subjects, major, and year
  • Session Management: Create, join, and manage study sessions with flexible scheduling
  • Community Ratings: Build trust through transparent peer ratings and reviews
  • Progress Tracking: Monitor your study activities and learning statistics
  • User-Friendly Interface: Clean, intuitive design for seamless experience

Technology Stack

  • Backend: FastAPI (Python)
  • Database: MongoDB
  • Frontend: HTML, CSS, JavaScript
  • Deployment: Cloud platforms (Render, Railway, etc.)

Why StudySync?

Studying alone can be isolating and less effective. StudySync makes collaborative learning accessible and enjoyable by connecting students with similar interests and goals. Whether you're preparing for exams, working on group projects, or just want to learn with others, StudySync provides the tools and community you need.
